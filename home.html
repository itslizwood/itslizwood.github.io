Who am I?

Education

I am a recent graduate of the Masters of Digital Media program at Ryerson University. My thesis explored facial recognition technology and prototyping for Android.

I also have a Bachelor of Technology from Ryerson University. I specialized in print technology, business management, marketing and graphic design.

Philosophy

I am a people-focused designer who practices human-centered design. Prototyping and UX design is where I aim to be.

I always strive to work on projects within the field of Advanced Wellbeing. With design principles and digital tools, I hope to bridge the divide between human emotion and technology.

When I'm not designing, you can find me planning weddings, baking, creating art, and watching lots and lots of YouTube.
